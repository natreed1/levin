              Privacy & data

              Understand where your information lives.

              Your Flyleaf data

              Account details, rooms, and messages are stored in Flyleaf’s database. Research sessions, events, and artifacts are kept in a separate directory for your account.

              Passwords are hashed. Connected model keys are encrypted before being written to disk. Flyleaf does not send ledger content to a model unless you explicitly start a model-backed action.
